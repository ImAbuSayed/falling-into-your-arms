[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Pouring shadows in this little room
The way you look at me just lights the gloom
I want to lose the world right here with you
And keep on falling like we always do

[Verse 1]
Midnight tapping on the windowpane
A warm October with the gentle rain
You lean a little closer to my side
And suddenly there’s nowhere left to hide

[Pre-Chorus]
The space between us fades into the dark
I trace the little lines across your mark
I hear your breathing steady in the glow
I’m holding on and never letting go

[Chorus]
Falling into your arms again
Right where the noise and the rushing end
You pull me closer and I close my eyes
Caught in the fire of a sweet surprise

[Post-Chorus]
Falling down, falling deep
Into the magic that we keep

[Verse 2]
I memorize the curve along your face
A memory that time cannot erase
Your fingers whisper softly on my skin
And I don’t even know where I begin

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The space between us fades into the dark
I trace the little lines across your mark
I hear your breathing steady in the glow
I’m holding on and never letting go

[Chorus]
Falling into your arms again
Right where the noise and the rushing end
You pull me closer and I close my eyes
Caught in the fire of a sweet surprise

[Post-Chorus 2]
Falling down, falling deep
Into the magic that we keep

[Bridge]
Tell me the secrets that you left behind
Show me the quiet where our hearts align
This is the moment that we’re meant to steal
Nothing but Saeed and the way you feel
Letting the rhythm of the shadows play
Chasing the edges of the light away

[Spoken]
[Whispered] (I just want to stay right here)
[Whispered] (Forever in your arms)

[Final Chorus]
Falling into your arms again
Right where the noise and the rushing end
You pull me closer and I close my eyes
Caught in the fire of a sweet surprise
